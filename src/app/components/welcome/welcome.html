This application is for Summer/Fall 2016 first-year applicants only. Transfer applicants and continuing Penn State students should contact the SHC for more information.

A complete application to Schreyer Honors College requires the following items:

  1. A complete application for regular admission to Penn State
  2. Answers to the Schreyer Honors College Application essay questions
  3. Answers to the supplemental questions
  4. Teacher Evaluations from two teachers selected by you
  5. A non-refundable application fee of $30

The priority application deadline for the SHC is November 30, 2016. All SHC applicants will be notified of their status by mid-March.
